What is DinnerPad?

DinnerPad is a service that makes it easy for Zambians to order delicious food from local restaurants and takeaways. Even from some that don't usually deliver!

You can browse the full menus and pick whatever you like from the comfort of your own home or office.

You can even try new restaurants and cuisines without having to get all dressed up.

Eat in style on your own terms.

How do I make an order?

It's really simple:

1. Choose your town/city.

2. Pick a restaurant and choose your food. ( make sure you reach minimum order amount for delivery. )

3. Place your order and wait for delivery to arrive.

4. Pay with Cash.

5. Enjoy!

NOTE

We aim to provide the best service possible so those who abuse the system by ordering and refusing to pay risk getting banned.
